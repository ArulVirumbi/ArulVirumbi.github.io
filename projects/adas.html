Lane Detection and Traffic sign Recognition

Developed a Driver Assistance System with Lane Detection and Traffic sign recognition. Used OpenCV library and LeNet model to classify the traffic sign. Implemented this system on Raspberry Pi 4, the system acquires the front view using a camera mounted on the vehicle and detects the lanes by localization of the road and determining the relative position between the vehicle and road. Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) are used to extract the features and classify traffic signs into corresponding classes.
