Driver Drowsiness Detection

  • Category: Machine Learning
  • Project timeline: Jun 2023
  • Project URL: Drowsiness Detection

Driver drowsiness detection is a computer vision application that aims to prevent accidents caused by fatigue or sleepiness of drivers. It uses OpenCV and shape predictor 68 to analyze the facial features of the driver and detect signs of drowsiness, such as low blink rate, droopy eyes and yawning. The system can alert the driver or take corrective actions when drowsiness is detected, thus improving road safety and saving lives.
