20 Seconds

  • Category: Android App
  • Project timeline: Jan - Feb 2023
  • Project URL: 20 Seconds

Android App to stop procrastination

20 Seconds is a productivity app designed to help you stop procrastination and stay on top of your tasks. With 20 Seconds, you can easily create a task list and receive pop-up notifications over other apps that show your current task, helping you stay mindful of your time and avoid distractions.

Whether you're a student, a professional, or anyone looking to boost their productivity, 20 Seconds can help you stay focused and on track. With 20 Seconds, you can take control of your time and get more done.
