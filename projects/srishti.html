Srishti Website for IEEE SC 12951

As the lead of a team of five members, I spearheaded the development of the Srishti website for IEEE Students Chapter 12951. This PHP and SQL-based platform showcased events, workshops, and paper presentations conducted in a technical symposium named Srishti, while also managing registrations for these events. I integrated the database and developed the registration system, along with implementing key features like the login/signup page, profile page, and integrated payment system.
The website garnered impressive traction, with over 2,000 paid registrations in a month.
